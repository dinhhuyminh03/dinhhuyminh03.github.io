About

All masters begin their paths the same way: make their first crafts. It may be ugly. It may be full of errors. But it is the most important thing, for the fruit never comes to life without a humble seed. And this website is exactly our beloved seed. Movie Center is our first project and our first step into the path of the seasoned web-makers. It was built to not just show you what we can be capable of but to serve as a great lesson for us as well. Because as you know, the best teacher in our life is ourselves. As the introduction comes to an end, I hope you like our first website. If you notice any flaw, please let us know so that we can improve and come closer to the goal we have set for ourselves.

Thank you for reading this and have a nice day!
